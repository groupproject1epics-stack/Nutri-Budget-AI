# 🍽️ Nutri-Budget AI Meal

## Project Overview
Nutri-Budget AI Meal is a smart meal planning website that helps users find food items within their budget while tracking calories and dietary preferences. It integrates live menus from popular food apps like Swiggy, Zomato, and Toing.

## Features
- ✅ **Budget Tracking** - Enter your budget in ₹ INR and see what you can afford
- ✅ **Calorie Calculator** - Select food items and see total calories instantly
- ✅ **Diet Filters** - Filter by Veg, Non-Veg, or Vegan options
- ✅ **Live Menus** - Browse menus from Swiggy, Zomato, Toing, and Nearby restaurants
- ✅ **Google Maps Integration** - Find nearby restaurants in India
- ✅ **Responsive Design** - Works on Mobile, Tablet, Laptop, and PC
- ✅ **Indian Rupees (₹)** - All prices in INR for Indian users

## How to Use
1. **Set Your Budget** - Enter your budget in the ₹ input field
2. **Choose a Diet** - Select Veg, Non-Veg, or Vegan
3. **Browse Menus** - Click on Swiggy, Zomato, Toing, or Nearby
4. **Select Food Items** - Click on any food card to select/deselect
5. **Track Totals** - See total calories and price at the bottom
6. **Find Restaurants** - Click "Nearby" to open Google Maps
